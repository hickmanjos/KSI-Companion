<b>Co-Division Leader</b><br/>
<b>Description:</b> This position is given to those Founders who may become eligible for a promotion after 24 weeks of training and experience in KSI.
<br><b>Requirements:</b> Successful fulfillment of the Founder position for at least a month’s time, and maintained a division with positive growth and member retention. Appointed for promotion by a Division Leader and approved by a Director.
<br><b>Duties:</b> Work with the Co-Founders and Founder to run and maintain a productive division, staying active on www.ksiforums.org, and should contribute to their division section while encouraging others to do the same. They solve major problems brought to them by the Founder. They act as a Division Leader when one is not present and represent their division at leaders meetings when the Division Leader is unavailable. They are responsible for suggesting the promotion or demotion of Founders or Co- Founders with the approval of the Division Leader and Director.
<br><b>Reports to:</b> Division Leader and Director or Senior Director.<br>
<b>Comments:</b> They assist in running the division by providing structure and ensuring an enjoyable gaming experience for all.
