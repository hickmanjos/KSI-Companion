<b>Description:</b> This position is given to those Sergeants who may become eligible for a promotion after 3 weeks of training and experience in KSI.
<br><b>Requirements:</b> Successful fulfillment of the Sergeant rank in the eyes of the squad leaders.
<br><b>Duties:</b> Helping their Captains with squad activities and maintaining squad lists. A lieutenant should know and understand how to run background checks on the new members. They must being fully trained in recruiting techniques and know the Code of Conduct and Rank Structure. They have to be active on www.ksiforums.org They are responsible for solving minor problems and recruiting new members.
<br><b>Reports to:</b> Captains and General.
<br><b>Comments:</b> They should be role models for Sergeants, Corporals and Privates.
