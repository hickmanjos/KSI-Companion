<b>Description:</b> The Staff Sergeant rank is used in those squads with a large amount of sergeants.<br/>
This position is given to those Sergeants who have proven themselves as future leaders of the squad but are not yet ready for LT because either lack of time or the LT positions are filled.<br/>
The position is appointed by the Generals with approval by Co-founder.
<br><b>Abbreviation:</b> SSGT<br><br><br><br><br><br>
